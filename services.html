Our Modern design.

Modern designs & renovations

Veterian blinds

Venetian blinds can be supplied in either aluminium or wood, all allowing you to easily control your privacy and and light entry, Aluminium blinds provide a sleek, modern and durable finish to your window and can be made in 15mm, 25mm or the fashionable 50mm

Roller blinds

Modern roller blinds offer a wide variety of materials from PVC to white canvas and other materials in a range of colours. They are versatile enough to be used with any décor style, and are completely unobtrusive when raised completely. The mechanisms are of superior quality, with smooth operation and longevity

roman blinds

Roman blinds provide privacy, insulation and complete light control. Inspired by the latest trends in soft furnishings, fabric roman blinds are hand finished to the highest standards. Suitable for any area of the home but also practical in offices and other commercial locations.

Curtaining

Curtains can come in a limitless number of different styles and fabrics that change consistently with fashion and trends, we specialise in creating the curtains to suit you and your personal space. Through the use of different fabrics, or combination of fabrics, we help you achieve the right look for you.

Home improvement + renovation + remodel

Our renovations team combines a passionate interior designer, innovative home improvement ideas and the talents of licenced contractors to provide commercial and residential remodelling services. Our home improvement services include conceptual ideas, designs, planning and building of kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, bedrooms and general remodelling.

renovation design

We want to ensure we capture your home improvement ideas and turn them into something magical. We draw every detail so by the end of the renovation design phase you have a realistic view of your home remodelling potential.

renovation planning

Our project management and supervision help you “stress less”. We take the time to plan the remodelling upfront and schedule all construction materials and products to ensure quality, style, service and satisfaction.

Renovation building

We team up with approved contractors on projects to implement and manage every stage of your home improvement. As a team we see value as a balance of price, service and quality. A good price without service and quality craftsmanship can become very expensive.
